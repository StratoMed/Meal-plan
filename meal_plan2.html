Weekly Family Meal Plan

Monday

Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs, Toast & Fruit

Ingredients: 4 eggs, a splash of milk, salt & pepper, a pat of butter, 4 slices of bread, strawberries & bananas (sliced).
Instructions: Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat, add eggs and stir until just set. Toast the bread and serve with fruit.

Lunch: Turkey & Cheese Wraps, Cucumber Slices & Applesauce

Ingredients: Flour tortillas, sliced turkey, cheese slices, lettuce, 1 sliced cucumber, applesauce.
Instructions: Layer turkey, cheese, and lettuce in a tortilla; roll tightly and slice. Serve with cucumber slices and applesauce.

Supper: Grilled Chicken, Mashed Potatoes & Roasted Carrots

Ingredients: 4 chicken breasts, olive oil, garlic powder, salt & pepper, 4 medium potatoes (peeled and quartered), butter, milk (for mashing), 4–5 carrots (cut into sticks), rosemary.
Instructions: Season chicken and grill until cooked. Boil potatoes, then mash with butter, milk, salt, and pepper. Toss carrots in olive oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary; roast in a 400°F oven for 20–25 minutes.
Tuesday

Breakfast: Oatmeal with Honey, Cinnamon & Blueberries

Ingredients: 1 cup rolled oats, 2 cups water or milk, 1 tbsp honey, a dash of cinnamon, 1/2 cup blueberries.
Instructions: Boil water/milk, cook oats until creamy, stir in honey and cinnamon, top with blueberries.

Lunch: DIY Lunchables

Ingredients: Cheese squares, crackers, sliced ham, baby carrots.
Instructions: Arrange ingredients on a plate for a fun deconstructed lunch.

Supper: Spaghetti with Meat Sauce & Garlic Bread

Ingredients: 12 oz spaghetti, 1 lb ground beef, 1 jar tomato sauce, 1 small diced onion, 2 cloves minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt & pepper, olive oil, garlic bread.
Instructions: Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil; brown ground beef then add tomato sauce and seasonings. Simmer for 15 minutes, cook spaghetti per package instructions, and serve with garlic bread.
Wednesday

Breakfast: Pancakes with Maple Syrup & Greek Yogurt

Ingredients: Pancake mix (or flour, baking powder, milk, egg, melted butter), maple syrup, Greek yogurt.
Instructions: Prepare batter and cook pancakes until golden; serve with syrup and Greek yogurt.

Lunch: Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwiches with Veggie Sticks

Ingredients: Bread, peanut butter, jelly, carrot & celery sticks.
Instructions: Make sandwiches and serve with veggie sticks.

Supper: Chicken Stir-Fry with Rice & Steamed Broccoli

Ingredients: 2 chicken breasts (sliced), mixed stir-fry vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, snap peas), 2 cloves garlic, a small piece of grated ginger, soy sauce, 2 cups cooked rice, 1 head steamed broccoli.
Instructions: Stir-fry chicken until nearly cooked; add garlic, ginger, and vegetables. Drizzle with soy sauce, cook until tender-crisp, and serve over rice with broccoli.
Thursday

Breakfast: Breakfast Burritos

Ingredients: Flour tortillas, 4 eggs, 1 cup shredded cheese, mild salsa, salt & pepper.
Instructions: Scramble eggs with cheese, season, spoon onto tortillas with salsa, and roll into burritos.

Lunch: Grilled Cheese Sandwiches & Tomato-Free Veggie Soup

Grilled Cheese Ingredients: Bread, cheese slices, butter.
Soup Ingredients: 4 cups vegetable broth, diced carrots, celery, 1 potato, a handful of green beans, salt & pepper.
Instructions: Grill buttered cheese sandwiches until melty. For soup, simmer vegetables in broth for about 20 minutes and season.

Supper: Steak, Baked Sweet Potatoes & Green Beans

Ingredients: 2 steaks, salt, pepper, olive oil, 2 sweet potatoes, 1 lb green beans.
Instructions: Season and cook steaks to desired doneness. Bake sweet potatoes at 400°F (pierced with a fork) for about 45 minutes; steam or sauté green beans.
Friday

Breakfast: Yogurt Parfaits with Granola & Mixed Berries

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, 1 cup granola, 1–2 cups mixed berries.
Instructions: Layer yogurt, granola, and berries in a cup.

Lunch: Mini Homemade Pizzas with Fruit Salad

Mini Pizzas Ingredients: English muffins or mini pizza crusts, 1/2 cup tomato sauce, 1–1½ cups shredded mozzarella, optional toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, olives).
Fruit Salad: Diced apple, grapes, kiwi, pineapple (or your favorite fruits).
Instructions: Split muffins, spread sauce, add cheese and toppings, and bake at 400°F for 8–10 minutes. Toss fruits for a salad.

Supper: Soft Tacos with Ground Beef, Cheese & Toppings

Ingredients: Soft taco shells, 1 lb ground beef, taco seasoning, shredded lettuce, 1 diced tomato, 1 cup shredded cheese, salsa, and sour cream.
Instructions: Brown beef, add taco seasoning with a splash of water and simmer for 5 minutes. Fill tacos with beef, lettuce, tomato, and cheese; top with salsa and sour cream.
Saturday

Breakfast: Boiled Eggs, Toast & Orange Slices

Ingredients: 4 eggs, 4 slices of bread, 2 oranges (peeled and sectioned).
Instructions: Boil eggs for 9–10 minutes; toast bread and serve with orange slices.

Lunch: Chicken Salad Sandwiches with Sliced Bell Peppers

Ingredients: 2 cups cooked shredded chicken, 1/3 cup mayonnaise, 1 chopped celery stalk, salt & pepper, 8 slices of bread, 2 sliced bell peppers.
Instructions: Mix chicken, mayo, celery, salt, and pepper; spread onto bread to make sandwiches and serve with bell peppers.

Supper: BBQ Chicken, Mac & Cheese & Coleslaw

BBQ Chicken: 4 chicken pieces (breasts or thighs) with 1 cup BBQ sauce.
Mac & Cheese: 8 oz macaroni, 2 cups milk, 2 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp flour, 2 cups shredded cheddar, salt & pepper.
Coleslaw: 1 bag coleslaw mix, coleslaw dressing.
Instructions: Bake BBQ-coated chicken at 375°F for 30–35 minutes. Prepare a roux with butter and flour, whisk in milk, add cheese and season; combine with macaroni. Toss coleslaw with dressing.
Sunday

Breakfast: Waffles with Syrup & Turkey Sausage

Ingredients: Waffle mix (or batter with flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, oil), maple syrup, turkey sausage links.
Instructions: Prepare waffles as directed; cook turkey sausage and serve with syrup.

Lunch: Quesadillas with Cheese & Beans, Served with Salsa

Ingredients: Flour tortillas, 1–1½ cups shredded cheese, 1 can black beans (drained), salsa.
Instructions: Sprinkle cheese and beans on half of each tortilla, fold, and cook in a skillet until golden and melty; serve with salsa.

Supper: Baked Pork Chops, Rice Pilaf & Steamed Peas

Ingredients: 4 pork chops, olive oil, salt, pepper, (optional thyme or rosemary), 2 cups rice, 2 cups broth, 1 cup peas.
Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F. Rub pork chops with olive oil and season. Bake for 20–25 minutes (until internal temperature reaches 145°F). Meanwhile, cook rice in broth and steam peas for about 5 minutes.
