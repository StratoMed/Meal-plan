Weekly Family Meal Plan & Recipes

Monday

Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs, Toast & Fruit

Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- A splash of milk
- Salt & pepper
- A pat of butter
- 4 slices of bread
- Strawberries & bananas (sliced)

Instructions:
1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat, add eggs and stir gently until just set.
3. Toast the bread and serve with a bowl of sliced strawberries and bananas.

Lunch: Turkey & Cheese Wraps, Cucumber Slices & Applesauce

Ingredients:
- Flour tortillas (4)
- Sliced turkey (8–12 slices)
- Cheese slices (4)
- Lettuce leaves
- 1 cucumber (sliced)
- Applesauce

Instructions:
1. Lay a tortilla flat, layer with turkey, cheese, and lettuce.
2. Roll tightly and slice in half.
3. Serve with cucumber slices and applesauce.

Supper: Grilled Chicken, Mashed Potatoes & Roasted Carrots

Ingredients:
- 4 chicken breasts
- Olive oil, garlic powder, salt & pepper
- 4 medium potatoes (peeled and quartered)
- 2–3 tbsp butter & a splash of milk (for mashing)
- 4–5 carrots (cut into sticks)
- A sprinkle of rosemary

Instructions:
1. Season chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Grill until fully cooked.
2. Boil potatoes until tender, then mash with butter, milk, salt, and pepper.
3. Toss carrots with olive oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary; roast in a 400°F (200°C) oven for 20–25 minutes.

Tuesday

Breakfast: Oatmeal with Honey, Cinnamon & Blueberries

Ingredients:
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 cups water or milk
- 1 tbsp honey
- A dash of cinnamon
- 1/2 cup blueberries

Instructions:
1. Bring water or milk to a boil, add oats and cook until creamy.
2. Stir in honey and cinnamon, then top with blueberries.

Lunch: DIY Lunchables

Ingredients:
- Sliced cheese (cut into squares)
- Crackers
- Sliced ham (6–8 slices)
- A small pack of baby carrots

Instructions:
1. Arrange cheese, crackers, and ham on a plate.
2. Serve with baby carrots.

Supper: Spaghetti with Meat Sauce & Garlic Bread

Ingredients:
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 jar tomato sauce
- 1 small onion (diced)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- Italian seasoning, salt & pepper
- Olive oil
- Garlic bread (store-bought or homemade from a baguette with garlic butter)

Instructions:
1. Sauté diced onion and garlic in olive oil until soft, add ground beef and cook until browned.
2. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning; simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Cook spaghetti as directed; serve with meat sauce and warmed garlic bread.

Wednesday

Breakfast: Pancakes with Maple Syrup & Greek Yogurt

Ingredients:
- Pancake mix (or flour, baking powder, milk, egg, melted butter)
- Maple syrup
- Greek yogurt

Instructions:
1. Prepare pancake batter following package or recipe instructions.
2. Cook pancakes on a hot griddle until golden.
3. Serve with maple syrup and a side of Greek yogurt.

Lunch: Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwiches with Veggie Sticks

Ingredients:
- Bread (8 slices)
- Peanut butter
- Jelly
- Carrot and celery sticks

Instructions:
1. Spread peanut butter and jelly on bread, assemble sandwiches, and cut in halves.
2. Serve with carrot and celery sticks.

Supper: Chicken Stir-Fry with Rice & Steamed Broccoli

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts (sliced into strips)
- Stir-fry vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, snap peas)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- A small piece of ginger (grated)
- 2–3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 1 head broccoli (steamed)

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat, add chicken strips and stir-fry until nearly cooked.
2. Add garlic, ginger, and vegetables; stir-fry until tender-crisp, then drizzle with soy sauce.
3. Serve over rice with a side of steamed broccoli.

Thursday

Breakfast: Breakfast Burritos

Ingredients:
- Flour tortillas (4)
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- Mild salsa
- Salt & pepper

Instructions:
1. Scramble eggs with salt and pepper, then stir in cheese until melted.
2. Spoon the egg mixture onto tortillas, add salsa, and roll into burritos.

Lunch: Grilled Cheese Sandwiches & Tomato-Free Veggie Soup

For Grilled Cheese:
- Bread (8 slices)
- Cheese slices (4–6)
- Butter

Instructions:
1. Butter one side of each slice; place cheese between two slices (buttered sides out) and grill until golden and melty.

For Veggie Soup:
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 2 carrots, 2 celery stalks, 1 potato (diced)
- A handful of green beans (trimmed)
- Salt & pepper

Instructions:
1. Combine all vegetables and broth in a pot, simmer until tender (about 20 minutes), then season.

Supper: Steak, Baked Sweet Potatoes & Green Beans

Ingredients:
- 2 steaks (your preferred cut)
- Salt, pepper & olive oil
- 2 sweet potatoes
- 1 lb green beans

Instructions:
1. Season steaks with salt, pepper, and olive oil; grill or pan-sear to your desired doneness.
2. Pierce sweet potatoes with a fork and bake at 400°F (200°C) for about 45 minutes until soft.
3. Steam or sauté green beans with olive oil and salt.

Friday

Breakfast: Yogurt Parfaits with Granola & Mixed Berries

Ingredients:
- Greek yogurt (1–2 cups)
- 1 cup granola
- 1–2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions:
1. Layer yogurt, granola, and berries in cups or bowls.

Lunch: Mini Homemade Pizzas with Fruit Salad

Mini Pizzas Ingredients:
- English muffins or mini pizza crusts (4)
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 1–1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- Optional: pepperoni, bell peppers, olives

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Split muffins, spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese and add toppings.
2. Bake for 8–10 minutes until bubbly.

Fruit Salad Ingredients:
- Diced apple, grapes, kiwi, pineapple (or your favorite fruits)

Instructions:
1. Toss fruits together in a bowl; chill if desired.

Supper: Soft Tacos with Ground Beef, Cheese & Toppings

Ingredients:
- Soft taco shells (8)
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade mix)
- Shredded lettuce
- 1 tomato (diced)
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- Salsa & sour cream

Instructions:
1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet; drain excess fat and stir in taco seasoning with a splash of water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Fill taco shells with beef, lettuce, tomato, and cheese; top with salsa and sour cream.

Saturday

Breakfast: Boiled Eggs, Toast & Orange Slices

Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 4 slices of bread
- 2 oranges (peeled and sectioned)

Instructions:
1. Boil eggs for about 9–10 minutes for hard-cooked eggs.
2. Toast the bread and serve with orange slices.

Lunch: Chicken Salad Sandwiches with Sliced Bell Peppers

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 1 celery stalk (finely chopped)
- Salt & pepper
- 8 slices of bread
- 2 bell peppers (sliced)

Instructions:
1. Combine chicken, mayonnaise, celery, salt, and pepper.
2. Spread onto bread slices to make sandwiches; serve with sliced bell peppers.

Supper: BBQ Chicken, Mac & Cheese & Coleslaw

BBQ Chicken Ingredients:
- 4 chicken pieces (breasts or thighs)
- 1 cup BBQ sauce

Mac & Cheese Ingredients:
- 8 oz macaroni
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- Salt & pepper

Coleslaw Ingredients:
- 1 bag pre-made coleslaw mix
- Coleslaw dressing (or mayo with a splash of vinegar)

Instructions:
1. Coat chicken with BBQ sauce; bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–35 minutes until cooked through.
2. Cook macaroni; prepare a roux with butter and flour, whisk in milk until thick, then stir in cheese, salt, and pepper; combine with macaroni.
3. Toss coleslaw mix with dressing.

Sunday

Breakfast: Waffles with Syrup & Turkey Sausage

Ingredients:
- Waffle mix or batter (flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, oil)
- Maple syrup
- Turkey sausage links

Instructions:
1. Prepare waffles as directed and cook in a waffle iron until golden.
2. Heat turkey sausage links and serve alongside waffles with syrup.

Lunch: Quesadillas with Cheese & Beans, Served with Salsa

Ingredients:
- Flour tortillas (4)
- 1–1½ cups shredded cheese
- 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
- Salsa (for dipping)

Instructions:
1. Sprinkle cheese and black beans on one half of each tortilla, fold and cook in a skillet until golden and melted.
2. Serve with salsa.

Supper: Baked Pork Chops, Rice Pilaf & Steamed Peas

Ingredients:
- 4 pork chops (bone-in or boneless)
- Olive oil, salt, pepper, and optional herbs (thyme or rosemary)
- 2 cups rice (white or brown)
- 2 cups broth (chicken or vegetable)
- 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Rub pork chops with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
2. Arrange on a baking tray and bake for 20–25 minutes (until internal temperature reaches 145°F).
3. Meanwhile, cook rice in broth per package directions.
4. Steam peas for about 5 minutes. Serve together.
